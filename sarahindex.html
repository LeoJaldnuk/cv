You are my World

My Dearest Sarita ,

You are my everything!

Your right eye is the Sun, which shines on me a radiant warmth,

And your left eye is the Moon, which winks at me with a secret smile.

Your body grounds me, it is my Earth which I long to hold fast to.

The Milky Way is dull next to your shimmering hair,

And you take my breath away such that I see stars all around you.

In your mind I know there is an infinite expanse of wonder,

But in your heart,

Oh your lovely beating heart!

In your heart I hear the pulse of all peoples,

I sense the unending hope for the future ,

I feel the flow of great faith,

But most of all I am moved by the beating of your heart,

That which moves the ruby red brilliance of your life blood:

The love that makes your heart beat.

And with each beat of your heart, my heart beats too,

And with each breath I see the world anew.

My world! My love!

My Sarita!
